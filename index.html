Project Aashnaa is a group of undergraduates working towards bettering the mental health of students who are struggling emotionally and mentally because of societal pressures, hyped up parental expectations and lack of career counseling. 



We are a non profit organization of volunteers and our aim is to create awareness in the people regarding the day-to-day struggle a student has to go through with the assurance that we are a safe place to connect with and talk to.
